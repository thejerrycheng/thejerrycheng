### 🚀 About My Work

I engineer generalized, highly articulate robots for the home. My approach is vertically integrated: from designing custom mechatronics to training robust visuo-motor policies directly on real-world data. 

<details>
  <summary><b>🛠️ Full-Stack Co-Design & Manipulation</b> <i>(Click to expand)</i></summary>
  <br>
  I strongly believe that the best robotic systems mitigate the embodiment gap by co-designing hardware, sensing, and control. I focus heavily on <b>contact-rich dexterous manipulation</b> using high-degree-of-freedom anthropomorphic systems (like 17-21 DOF hands, ORCA, and LEAP). 
  <br><br>
  For low-level control, I develop precise RL policies, currently utilizing an absolute action space, which I've found consistently outperforms incremental baselines for bridging semantic high-level reasoning and fine-grained actuation.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>📡 Scalable Data & Teleoperation Pipelines</b> <i>(Click to expand)</i></summary>
  <br>
  To capture high-fidelity human demonstrations for policy training, I build advanced spatial computing tools. My data collection pipelines heavily leverage <b>Apple Vision Pro and MediaPipe</b> to teleoperate complex robotic hands and stream video directly into the learning stack.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>🗺️ Perception & Navigation</b> <i>(Click to expand)</i></summary>
  <br>
  Unified controllers require certifiable, multimodal perception. My background includes building active SLAM pipelines (implementing FastSLAM with frontier exploration and A* planning) and globally optimal radar-camera calibration to ensure reliable embodied control.
</details>

### 🤖 Current Builds & Selected Projects

* 🦾 **MABEL (Wall-E):** A mobile bimanual dexterous robot I am currently building from the swerve-drive base up, specifically designed for household chores. The platform integrates custom high-discharge Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) battery chemistry for safe, sustained domestic operation. (Targeting an RA-L systems paper submission by mid-August).
* 🎥 **IRIS (CRV 2026):** A task-specific, low-cost 3D-printed 6-DOF cinema robot arm. It utilizes goal-conditioned transformer-based imitation learning to autonomously generate smooth, object-aware camera motion.
* 📦 **GeoDEX:** Diffusion-transformer policy for 26-DOF dexterous pick-and-place, mapping dual-view RGBD and proprioception to action chunks with SE(3) geometric regularization.
* 🥽 **AeroHaptix (IEEE RA-L 2024):** A spatialized wearable vibrotactile feedback system leveraging control barrier functions for safe human-drone interaction.
